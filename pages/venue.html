Venue

Chowder Bay, Sydney Harbour

Located at the northern precinct of Sydney Harbour National Park, Chowder Bay is named after whalers who made chowder from the bay's seafood. It is a popular spot for swimming and picnicking. There are large grassy areas and spectacular harbour views.

Chowder Bay is approximately 25 km from Sydney Airport. The estimated Taxi fare is $70.
